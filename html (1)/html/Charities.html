Our Charities

Burrito Boyz

§ Since 2010, Burrito Boyz has been serving the homeless population in San Diego. With the help of volunteers of all ages, the organization brings hot meals to the homeless every Sunday. They have been doing this for more than 500 weeks, serving over 255,000 burritos. They are committed to inspiring youth to take action in their community.

Kitchens for Good

They provide free apprenticeship programs on culinary, baking, and hospitality expertise; in doing so they help many create sustainable careers. They give their students weekly stipends to cover costs, transportation support, and an unending supply of TLC. Their mission is to help the underprivileged stay off the streets and make an honest living.

Mama’s Kitchen

Mama’s Kitchen strives to provide nutritional support to San Diego residents at risk of malnutrition due to critical illnesses such as HIV, cancer, congestive heart failure, type 2 diabetes, and chronic kidney disease. Together with hundreds of volunteers, businesses, and community supporters, Mama’s Kitchen strives to help our clients stay healthy, preserve their dignity, and keep their families together by providing, medically tailored home-delivered meals, along with pantry services and nutrition education – all at no cost.

San Diego Food Bank

The Jacobs & Cushman San Diego Food Bank and our North County Food Bank chapter provide nutritious food to people in need, advocate for the hungry, and educate the public about hunger-related issues.

Streets of Hope

The Streets of Hope mission is to build a community where those who have much can serve and give to those who have little because we believe that real transformation happens through relationship in community.
