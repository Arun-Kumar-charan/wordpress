Our Story

AJ Designs seeks to help the homeless and the hungry, improve literacy and education, and protect the environment. Our vision is to help create a more equal society and we aim to leave the world a better place for the next generations.

We make pieces in our Woodworking Studio and donate them to charities we support so they can auction them off for funds they put to use for their causes.

Our Charities

You can choose which local San Diego Charity your profits go to. We have selected charities focused on addressing food insecurity and the helping homeless

Burrito Boyz

Since 2010, Burrito Boyz has been serving the homeless population in San Diego. With the help of volunteers of all ages, the organization brings hot meals to the homeless every Sunday.

Learn More

Kitchens for Good

They provide free apprenticeship programs on culinary, baking, and hospitality expertise; in doing so they help many create sustainable careers.

Learn More

Mama’s Kitchen

Mama’s Kitchen strives to provide nutritional support to San Diego residents at risk of malnutrition due to critical illnesses such as HIV, cancer, congestive heart failure, type 2 diabetes, and chronic kidney disease.
